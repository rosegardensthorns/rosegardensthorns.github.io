The 58th Grammy Awards

15th February, 2016

Last night was the 58th annual Grammy Awards. Although Selena, Beyoncé and Adele did not win any Grammy's, Adele performed one of her songs. However, Taylor Swift both opened the show with her heartfelt, beautiful song 'Out of the Woods'. Taylor Swift also won three Grammy's- Best Pop Vocal Album, Best Music Video, and Album of the Year.
